General

What is Makhana?
Makhana, also known as fox nuts or lotus seeds, is a nutritious snack harvested from lotus seeds and consumed as a healthy superfood.
Is Makhana healthy for daily consumption?
Yes, makhana is rich in protein, fiber, calcium, and antioxidants, making it a healthy snack for daily consumption.
Is Makhana a superfood?
Yes, makhana is considered a superfood due to its high nutritional value and health benefits.
Why is makhana so expensive?
Makhana processing is labor-intensive and requires skilled roasting, which increases its premium pricing.

Health

Can Makhana help in weight loss?
Yes, makhana is low in calories and high in protein and fiber, making it ideal for weight loss diets.
Is makhana good for diabetes?
Makhana has a low glycemic index and helps regulate blood sugar levels.
Is makhana gluten-free?
Yes, makhana is naturally gluten-free and suitable for gluten-free diets.
Is makhana good for heart health?
Yes, makhana is low in cholesterol and sodium, making it heart-friendly.
Is makhana good for gym and fitness?
Yes, makhana is a great pre-workout and post-workout snack due to its protein and energy content.
Is makhana suitable for vegan and vegetarian diets?
Yes, plain makhana is naturally plant-based and suitable for vegetarian and vegan-friendly food routines.

Product

Which is the best quality makhana?
6+ size premium makhana is considered the best quality based on size and crunchiness.
What does 5+ or 6+ makhana mean?
It refers to the size grading of makhana. Higher numbers indicate bigger and better-quality seeds.
How to identify good quality makhana?
Premium makhana is white, crunchy, large-sized, and free from black spots.
How should makhana be stored?
Store makhana in an airtight container in a cool, dry place to maintain freshness.
What is the shelf life of makhana?
When packed correctly in moisture-resistant pouches and stored in a cool dry place, makhana generally has a shelf life of 6 to 12 months depending on product type.

Export and B2B

Do you export makhana internationally?
Yes, premium quality makhana can be exported worldwide including USA, UAE, Australia, and Europe.
What is MOQ for makhana export?
MOQ depends on packaging and country requirements but usually starts from 50kg to 500kg.
Do you provide private label makhana?
Yes, private label and white-label makhana packaging options are available for global buyers.
What packaging options are available for export?
Bulk packaging, retail packaging, and private label packaging options are available.
Do you provide lab reports or quality certifications for export buyers?
Yes, buyers can request relevant certification and quality documents based on destination country requirements and order category.
Can I get flavored and plain makhana in the same bulk order?
Yes, mixed-SKU orders are generally possible based on MOQ and packaging plan, allowing buyers to build a broader product range.
How long does export dispatch usually take after order confirmation?
Dispatch timeline depends on order size, packaging type, and destination; standard export shipments are planned after quality checks and document completion.
Which countries have the highest demand for Indian makhana?
Demand is growing strongly in the USA, UAE, UK, Australia, and other health-conscious snack markets.

Cooking

How to roast makhana at home?
Roast makhana in ghee or butter on low flame for 5 to 7 minutes and add spices.
Can makhana be eaten raw?
It is recommended to roast makhana before eating for better taste and crunch.
What dishes can be made from makhana?
Makhana kheer, roasted snacks, curry, trail mix, and flavored makhana can be prepared.
