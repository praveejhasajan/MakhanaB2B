How It Works

Your Brand on Shelves
in 4 Simple Steps

From your brief to customs clearance in your country — we handle manufacturing, compliance, and documentation. You focus on selling.

01
Share Your Brief
Tell us: your brand name, target market (USA, UK, UAE, etc.), preferred flavours, packaging size (40g, 100g, 250g, 1kg), and monthly volume estimate. WhatsApp or email us your requirements.
02
We Design & Sample
Our packaging team creates a mockup of your branded product. We send physical samples for your approval before any production runs. Feedback loop is typically 5–7 working days.
03
Confirm & Produce
Approve the sample, confirm your order quantity and incoterm (FOB/CIF), pay your advance, and production begins. Your private label makhana is packed and quality-tested within 10–14 days.
04
Ship With Full Docs
We ship your branded product with all export documents — commercial invoice, packing list, phytosanitary certificate, COO, and your market-specific compliance docs (FDA, FSA, ESMA, FSANZ).
